# ⚔️ Fantasy Class Skills System - COMPLETE! ✨

## 🎯 Overview:
**18 Unique Skills** across 6 fantasy character classes, each with distinct themes, mechanics, and strategic applications. Skills are designed to enhance class identity and provide diverse gameplay options.

## 🛡️ KNIGHT - The Stalwart Defender
*"Honor, duty, and unwavering protection guide the Knight's blade."*

### 1. **Shield Wall** 
- **Type**: Active
- **Description**: The Knight raises their shield, creating an impenetrable barrier that absorbs incoming damage while reflecting a portion back to attackers.
- **Effect**: 
  - Blocks next 3 attacks completely
  - Reflects 50% damage back to attacker
  - Duration: 3 turns or until 3 attacks blocked
  - Cooldown: 5 turns

### 2. **Guardian's Resolve**
- **Type**: Passive
- **Description**: The Knight's unwavering determination grows stronger as allies fall. For each defeated party member, the Knight gains increased defense and damage resistance.
- **Effect**:
  - +3 Defense per defeated ally
  - +10% damage resistance per defeated ally
  - Effect persists until combat ends
  - Maximum: +9 Defense, +30% resistance

### 3. **Righteous Strike**
- **Type**: Active
- **Description**: A powerful overhead strike infused with divine justice. Deals massive damage and has a chance to stun enemies, with increased effectiveness against evil creatures.
- **Effect**:
  - 200% weapon damage
  - 30% chance to stun for 1 turn
  - +50% damage vs undead/demons
  - Cooldown: 4 turns

## 🏹 ARCHER - The Precision Marksman
*"Every arrow finds its mark, every shot tells a story."*

### 1. **Piercing Shot**
- **Type**: Active
- **Description**: A masterfully aimed arrow that pierces through multiple enemies in a line, dealing reduced damage to each subsequent target but ignoring armor.
- **Effect**:
  - Hits up to 3 enemies in line
  - 100% → 75% → 50% damage progression
  - Ignores all armor/defense
  - Cooldown: 3 turns

### 2. **Hunter's Mark**
- **Type**: Passive
- **Description**: The Archer's keen eye marks wounded prey. Attacks against enemies below 50% health deal critical damage and have increased accuracy.
- **Effect**:
  - +100% crit chance vs enemies <50% HP
  - +25% accuracy vs marked targets
  - Critical hits reduce cooldowns by 1 turn
  - Always active

### 3. **Explosive Arrow**
- **Type**: Active
- **Description**: A specially crafted arrow that explodes on impact, dealing area damage and applying burning effects to all enemies in the blast radius.
- **Effect**:
  - 150% weapon damage to primary target
  - 75% damage to adjacent enemies
  - Applies Burn: 10 damage/turn for 3 turns
  - Cooldown: 5 turns

## 🔮 MAGE - The Arcane Weaver
*"Reality bends to the will of those who understand its true nature."*

### 1. **Arcane Missiles**
- **Type**: Active
- **Description**: Unleashes a barrage of magical projectiles that automatically seek the weakest enemies, dealing increasing damage with each successive hit.
- **Effect**:
  - Fires 5 missiles automatically
  - Each missile: 40% spell power
  - Damage increases by 20% per missile (40% → 60% → 80% → 100% → 120%)
  - Targets lowest HP enemies first
  - Cooldown: 4 turns

### 2. **Mana Shield**
- **Type**: Passive
- **Description**: The Mage's magical energy forms a protective barrier. Incoming damage is absorbed by mana before affecting health, but at an increased cost.
- **Effect**:
  - Damage absorbed by mana at 2:1 ratio
  - When mana depletes, +50% damage taken
  - Regenerate 5 mana per turn
  - Always active

### 3. **Elemental Mastery**
- **Type**: Active
- **Description**: The Mage channels raw elemental forces, cycling through Fire, Ice, and Lightning with each cast, applying different effects based on the element.
- **Effect**:
  - **Fire**: 180% spell damage + Burn (15 damage/3 turns)
  - **Ice**: 120% spell damage + Freeze (skip next turn)
  - **Lightning**: 150% spell damage + Chain (hits 2 additional enemies)
  - Cycles through elements in order
  - Cooldown: 3 turns

## ⚔️ BARBARIAN - The Primal Berserker
*"Rage is not madness—it is the purest form of strength unleashed."*

### 1. **Berserker Rage**
- **Type**: Active
- **Description**: The Barbarian enters a primal fury, gaining massive attack bonuses and damage resistance, but losing the ability to use items or defensive actions.
- **Effect**:
  - +100% attack damage
  - +50% damage resistance
  - Cannot use items or defend
  - +1 extra attack per turn
  - Duration: 4 turns
  - Cooldown: 6 turns

### 2. **Bloodthirst**
- **Type**: Passive
- **Description**: Each enemy slain fuels the Barbarian's bloodlust. Killing blows restore health and grant temporary attack bonuses that stack.
- **Effect**:
  - Heal 25% max HP on kill
  - +5 attack damage per kill (stacks)
  - Bonus damage lasts until combat ends
  - Maximum: +25 attack damage
  - Always active

### 3. **Earthquake Slam**
- **Type**: Active
- **Description**: A devastating ground pound that shakes the earth, dealing damage to all enemies and having a chance to knock them down, preventing actions.
- **Effect**:
  - 150% weapon damage to all enemies
  - 40% chance to knock down (lose next turn)
  - Destroys environmental obstacles
  - Self-damage: 10% current HP
  - Cooldown: 5 turns

## 🗡️ ASSASSIN - The Shadow Dancer
*"Death comes not with fanfare, but with silence and precision."*

### 1. **Shadow Step**
- **Type**: Active
- **Description**: The Assassin melts into shadows, becoming untargetable while positioning for a devastating backstab attack that deals massive critical damage.
- **Effect**:
  - Become untargetable for 1 turn
  - Next attack: 300% weapon damage
  - Guaranteed critical hit
  - Ignores enemy defensive abilities
  - Cooldown: 4 turns

### 2. **Poison Mastery**
- **Type**: Passive
- **Description**: All attacks have a chance to apply deadly toxins. Poisoned enemies take damage over time, and the Assassin gains bonuses when fighting poisoned foes.
- **Effect**:
  - 35% chance to poison on attack
  - Poison: 12 damage/turn for 4 turns
  - +25% damage vs poisoned enemies
  - +15% crit chance vs poisoned enemies
  - Always active

### 3. **Thousand Cuts**
- **Type**: Active
- **Description**: A flurry of lightning-fast strikes that hit multiple times, with each successful hit increasing the speed and damage of subsequent attacks.
- **Effect**:
  - 5 attacks at 60% weapon damage each
  - Each hit increases next hit damage by 20%
  - Final hit guaranteed critical if all connect
  - 25% chance each hit applies poison
  - Cooldown: 6 turns

## ✨ CLERIC - The Divine Conduit
*"Through faith and compassion, miracles become reality."*

### 1. **Divine Healing**
- **Type**: Active
- **Description**: Channels divine energy to restore health and remove all negative effects from the target, with healing power increased based on the target's missing health.
- **Effect**:
  - Heal 40% max HP + 1% per 1% missing HP
  - Removes all debuffs and status effects
  - Grants immunity to debuffs for 2 turns
  - Can target self or allies
  - Cooldown: 3 turns

### 2. **Blessed Aura**
- **Type**: Passive
- **Description**: The Cleric radiates divine protection, providing constant regeneration and resistance bonuses to all nearby allies, with effects strengthening in dire situations.
- **Effect**:
  - All allies regenerate 5 HP/turn
  - +2 Defense to all allies
  - Effects double when any ally below 25% HP
  - +10% resistance to all damage types
  - Always active (area effect)

### 3. **Wrath of Heaven**
- **Type**: Active
- **Description**: Calls down divine judgment upon enemies, dealing holy damage that increases based on the number of evil acts they've committed, while healing allies.
- **Effect**:
  - 120% spell power holy damage to all enemies
  - +50% damage vs undead/demons
  - Simultaneously heals all allies for 30% damage dealt
  - Chance to blind enemies (20% miss chance for 3 turns)
  - Cooldown: 5 turns

## 🎮 Skill System Mechanics:

### **Skill Types:**
- **Active Skills**: Require manual activation, have cooldowns
- **Passive Skills**: Always active, provide constant benefits

### **Cooldown System:**
- Skills have turn-based cooldowns
- Some effects can reduce cooldowns (crits, kills, etc.)
- Cooldowns reset between combat encounters

### **Synergy Design:**
- Skills within each class complement each other
- Passive skills enhance active skill effectiveness
- Multiple viable build paths per class

### **Balance Considerations:**
- High-power skills have longer cooldowns
- Risk/reward mechanics (Barbarian self-damage, Mage mana shield)
- Situational effectiveness (Knight vs evil, Archer vs wounded)

## 🎯 Strategic Depth:

### **Class Identity Reinforcement:**
- **Knight**: Defensive utility, protection focus
- **Archer**: Precision, tactical positioning
- **Mage**: Elemental mastery, resource management
- **Barbarian**: High-risk aggression, momentum-based
- **Assassin**: Stealth, burst damage, status effects
- **Cleric**: Support, healing, divine magic

### **Tactical Considerations:**
- **Timing**: When to use powerful cooldown abilities
- **Resource Management**: Mana, health, positioning
- **Synergy**: Combining skills for maximum effect
- **Adaptation**: Different skills for different enemy types
